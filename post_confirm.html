Nice bike. For age this is in great condition and as such it was even hard to identify the year! I believe this is a 2006 judging by the fork and shock but year may not be exact and would need to be clarified with manufacture. The frame has limited cosmetic wear with just a couple of minor scuffs but nothing major. Fork is similar with some cosmetic wear on lowers but stanchions look great. Maintenance records on fork and shock unknown and they may need to be serviced soon due to age, although there are no signs of oil leaking or damage. The wheels are great with no major damage or wear. The tires still have 50% life left but there is some wear to the sidewalls. Drive train is clean and free of excessive wear. There are no major cosmetic problems on the components and the only component with more age are the grips and saddle which have some weathering and cracking from age. Bike is ready to ride as is, fully functional, with lots of life left.
